Transylvanian Design Week

the context

the client

Transylvanian Design Week is a dynamic and comprehensive creative initiative that seeks to engage both creators and the general public in defining the status of contemporary Transylvanian design. Their mission is to support youth empowerment and social engagement through modern techniques and tools, creating opportunities for Transylvanian artists to connect and share their work with a wider audience.

visit website

the goal

For the first edition of Transylvanian Design Week the biggest challenge was to create the identity without trying to make any statements about what (we think) Transylvanian design means. Based on the central theme of the event, we created a design that visually reflects the ambitions of Transylvanian Design Week. Visually, we were inspired by the idea of collecting design ‘thought fragments’ starting from the announcement of the TWD competition until the opening of its main event.

The website and social media platforms are also based on this: both are initially a blank sheet, inviting you to add your perspective. Transylvanian Design Week will be what Transylvanian designers put together.

my role

As a UX designer and Webflow developer, my role was to refine the user experience and implement the design with pixel-perfect precision.
