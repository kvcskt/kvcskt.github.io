the client

For a few days in August, Méra is at the center of the world. The World Music Festival of Transylvania celebrates people, music, different cultures and stories. Here you can meet the very best of World Music on the Barn Stage.
The goal is to show the magic of the Kalotaszeg region to the world, while bringing bits of the whole world into the backyards of Mera villagers.
